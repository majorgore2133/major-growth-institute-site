Repair

Why “Repair” matters: Payment history is the biggest score factor. Removing lates/collections/charge-offs reduces risk flags and can move you into higher approval tiers with better pricing. If you can’t delete, aim to re-age or add strong new positives to dilute.

Build

Why “Build” matters:
Utilization
<10% signals prime behavior and high capacity. Models directly reward low balance-to-limit ratios.
Mix
≥3 revolving + ≥1 installment shows maturity lenders want to see, reducing denial risk on new lines.
Age
≥36 months average age dampens “new credit” penalties. Keep oldest lines open; avoid closing young subprime lines too early.
Depth
8–12 positive accounts gives a thick file without looking reckless. Too few = thin file; too many new = credit seeking.
