How it works

Designed for legitimacy and scale
  1. Audit: Our AI analyzes credit files and flags high-impact disputes and reporting errors.
  2. Plan: Compliance team builds a conservative remediation roadmap (disputes, tradelines, reporting fixes).
  3. Execute: We submit vetted disputes, manage creditor communications, and layer credit-building products.
  4. Fund: Once eligible, we move into funding sequences to access capital — always within legal and compliance boundaries.

What about M2 Compliance?

We partner with compliance and tech providers such as M2 Compliance to ensure our methods follow consumer protection laws and reduce risk. Our video explains the partnership at a high level — it is intentionally non-actionable.

Is this legal?

Yes. We use legally permissible dispute & remediation channels and never recommend fraudulent actions. Every client signs a compliance addendum.
